## How to get on this list
Want to see your name here? You can contribute by:
1. **Bug Hunting:** Reporting issues or providing logs when the mod crashes.
2. **Coding:** Submitting a Pull Request with improvements to the core logic.

*Contributors are added to this list upon their first accepted submission.*
